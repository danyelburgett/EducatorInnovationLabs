01

Staff Morale & Resilience Building

Perfect for K-8 & High School Administration

The Weak Prompt
"Help me with staff morale."

Why this fails: The AI has no clue what resources you have, what context your staff is in, who you're talking to, or what exact deliverable fits your upcoming meeting schedule.

The CLEAR Engineered Prompt

CONTEXT: I am a K-8 principal at a Title I urban school with 420 students and 35 staff. It's November and we've had three staff resignations and a string of difficult parent incidents that have shaken morale.

LENS: Act as an experienced school leadership coach who specializes in building resilient school cultures in high-need urban schools.

EXPECTATION: Design a 45-minute staff appreciation and re-energizing activity I can facilitate at our next faculty meeting to rebuild collective efficacy.

AUDIENCE: My audience is a mix of veteran teachers (10+ years) and newer teachers in years 1-2, many of whom are questioning whether they made the right career choice.

RESULT FORMAT: Give me a step-by-step facilitation guide with: a warm-up (10 min), main activity (25 min), and closing ritual (10 min). Include timing, materials, and one facilitator tip per section.
